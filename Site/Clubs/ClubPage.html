Mums and Babies

Mondays 11:00am - 1:30pm

This is a closed group run by ReAct. (ReAct was started in response to the tragedy unfolding in Syria). We at the start focused on sending donations abroad to Calais, Greece, and Iraq. Once the families began to arrive in Edinburgh through a government scheme, we started to work with them. Projects were commenced in response to what the community said they needed. From this a play group, a ladies’ group where English was taught, donation days where they could get what they needed and a welcome group where rucksacks were given to every new child. School packs for Primary 1 school children, winter coats were supplied and bikes for the children.

COVID 19 meant like thousands of people in Scotland we had to change how we did things. Ideas came from the community that they would like us to explore. One of these was the mums and babies’ group. This was to meet up once it was possible within safe limits. To socialise, to get information. Funding enabled us to start this group at Sandys Community centre for two years. This group is run by members of the community and ReAct.
